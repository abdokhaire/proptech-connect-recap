Key Takeaways

What This All Means

1

The UAE is the world's PropTech laboratory

With 1,080+ regional attendees and "Expanding to the UAE" as the #1 interest (599 selections), Dubai has cemented its position as the global testing ground for real estate technology.

2

Capital is following innovation

718 attendees with investment focus, 377 from PE/Family Office backgrounds, and 341 actively seeking startup deals. The money is ready to deploy.

3

AI is reshaping every layer of real estate

From Facade AI analyzing exteriors to Property GPT generating listings, from SearchSmartly matching buyers to Fixit AI managing maintenance — AI isn't coming. It's here.

4

Sustainability is non-negotiable

479 ESG-focused selections show green buildings, energy efficiency, and environmental compliance are core business requirements, not nice-to-haves.

5

Extraordinary founder density

184 founders in the room means PropTech is attracting serious entrepreneurial talent. Combined with investor presence, conditions for rapid innovation are perfect.

6

The full value chain is engaged

From AWS providing cloud infrastructure to Binghatti developing properties, from MRI Software managing portfolios to Engel & Völkers selling homes — every stakeholder is now a PropTech participant.

The future of real estate
is being built in tech.
